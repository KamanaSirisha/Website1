About FinPilot Students

FinPilot Students helps learners track money, discover student gigs and build healthy financial habits early. It is privacy-first — all data stored locally on your device.

Mission

Empower students to manage earnings and expenses, learn budgeting and find flexible ways to earn.

Features

  • Student-optimized categories (Pocket Money, Part-time Job, Scholarship)
  • Student Jobs & Services marketplace demo
  • Export/import CSV and local backups
